Learn how LearnITFast collects, uses, and protects information when you use the website, quizzes, flashcards, and learning tools.

We use collected information to keep the platform working properly and improve the learning experience.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact LearnITFast through the website’s contact page.
